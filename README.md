# ⚡ Energy Transition Intelligence  
## AI-Powered Investment Decision System  

> **Long Dongfang Electric (1072.HK) / Short Shanghai Electric (2727.HK)**  
> Multi-layer AI framework combining sentiment, macro signals, and factor screening  

---

## 🧩 Overview

This project builds an **AI-driven investment framework** to identify and validate a long–short trade in China’s energy transition sector.

It integrates **three independent AI modules** into a unified signal:

- 🧠 **NLP Sentiment Analysis** → market narrative  
- 🛰️ **Coal Displacement Signal** → macro transition  
- 📊 **Factor Screening Engine** → fundamentals  

👉 The result is a **converging multi-signal trade thesis**

---

## 🎯 Investment Thesis

> **Long Dongfang Electric / Short Shanghai Electric**

- Dongfang → beneficiary of nuclear, hydro, and grid upgrade cycle  
- Shanghai → exposed to governance issues and legacy thermal assets  

**AI signals consistently show:**
- Positive sentiment divergence  
- Structural energy transition tailwinds  
- Stronger financial positioning  

---

## 🧠 AI Architecture

### Layer 1 — NLP Sentiment Analysis
- Processes **46 real news headlines (25 sources)**
- Uses keyword-based financial NLP scoring  
- Clusters headlines into themes (e.g. nuclear, governance, exports)

**Output:**
- Sentiment Spread: **+0.84**
- Clear divergence in narrative momentum  

---

### Layer 2 — Coal Displacement Signal
- Uses publicly sourced data (Greenpeace, CEC, Carbon Brief)
- Applies:
  - Regression forecasting  
  - Correlation analysis  

**Key finding:**
- Coal utilization vs stock spread → **R² = 0.982**

👉 Strong statistical link between **energy transition and price divergence**

---

### Layer 3 — Factor Screening Engine
- Evaluates companies across **7 financial & strategic factors**
- Uses:
  - Min-max normalization  
  - Composite scoring  
  - Monte Carlo simulation (10,000 runs)

**Result:**
- Dongfang score: **73.4**
- Shanghai score: **22.2**
- Win rate: **100% across all weightings**

## 🧪 Methodology

### Composite Score Formula
```
Composite Score = Σ (Factor Score × Weight)
```

Factors include:
- Profitability (ROE, margins)
- Growth (order backlog)
- Balance sheet strength
- Energy transition exposure
